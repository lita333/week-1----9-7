My family is from Ecuador

Both my mother and father, (Madelaine + Fausto) were born in Quito, Ecuador .

On my maternal side , my grandmother (Abu Glady’s) is also from Quito but her mother (Marta), my great-grandmother, was from Ibarra. My grandmother’s father (Bolivar), my great-grandfather, is also from Quito, but his father (Ramon), (my great, great, grandfather) is from Cali, Colombia. My grandfather, (Abuelito Enrique) is also from Quito and so is his father (Edwardo), my great-grandfather. My grandfather’s mother on the other hand, my great-grandma (Antuquita), is from Ambato, Ecuador.

On my paternal side , my grandmother (Suzy) is from Quito but her mother, (Abuelita Quitita), my great-grandmother is from Ibarra and so is my great, great, grandmother (Celia). My dad’s father (Nestor) was also from Quito but that’s all we know about him. My father was fortunate to be raised only by the matriarchs of his family. And come to think of it, maybe that’s why he feels his emotions so deeply which is really rare for Ecuadorian men.
