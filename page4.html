My extremely hard working parents beat all the odds and were able to buy a home in Houston, Texas — Cypress, Texas to be exact.
They learned a new language, worked their ass off and persevered
They completely defeated all the obstacles most immigrants face and despite coming to this country with nothing — no family, no money, no language, they were able to give me and my brother a real home
A home where I had a real backyard A home where I had my own bedroom

No amount of thank-you’s or i love you can suffice for this type of sacrifice.

I am deeply grateful for how they protected me and tried their best to give me and my brother everything they never had
but Texas was my parents dream, not mine.

This is where my era of suburiba hell began
